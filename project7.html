NyaaySetu

AI-powered Indian Constitution platform for multilingual accessibility.

2026
Overview

NyaaySetu is an AI-powered platform designed to make the Indian Constitution accessible to every citizen by breaking language barriers. It simplifies complex legal jargon into easy-to-understand language and provides accurate translations across multiple Indian languages, making the vision of “Constitution for Everyone” a reality.

Key Features
  • Multilingual support (Hindi, English, Tamil, etc.)
  • AI-powered simplification of legal articles
  • Smart search for laws, articles, and topics
  • Interactive UI for better understanding of rights and duties
  • Accurate cross-language legal translations

The Challenge

Accurately translating complex legal terminology while maintaining the original legal meaning and context across diverse languages.

The Solution

Leveraged advanced NLP models and fine-tuned AI to provide context-aware translations and simplifications that remain legally sound.
