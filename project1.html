Aayan Art Glass

Personal shop static website for business showcase.

2025
Overview

Aayan Art Glass is a specialized static website designed to showcase artistic glass products, providing a digital presence for a personal business. The goal was to create a visually appealing catalog that highlights the artistry of each product while ensuring seamless performance on all devices.

Key Features
  • Responsive catalog display for all devices
  • Contact form integration for customer inquiries
  • Image gallery for art pieces with lightbox effect
  • SEO-optimized structure for local discovery

The Challenge

Creating a visually appealing layout that reflects the artistic nature of the products while keeping performance high on low-end devices.

The Solution

Used CSS Grid and clean Bootstrap components with lazy-loading images to ensure the art remains the focal point without sacrificing performance.
